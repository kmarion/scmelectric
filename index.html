Services

Residential

Residential

Keeping your home safe and powered

  • Ceiling Fan & Light Fixture Installation: Installation of new ceiling fans and light fixtures to enhance style and functionality in your home.
  • Electrical Panel Upgrades & Repairs: Upgrading your electrical panel to meet the increased demands of modern appliances or repairing existing panels.
  • Troubleshooting & Repair: Diagnose and fix electrical issues like faulty outlets, switches, or flickering lights.
  • Home Safety Inspections: Identify potential electrical hazards and recommend solutions to improve the overall safety of your home's electrical system.
  • Kitchen & Bathroom Remodel Electrical: Electrical wiring and fixture installation for kitchen and bathroom remodels, ensuring functionality and code compliance.

Commercial

No job is too big

  • Building Wiring & Electrical Panel Upgrades: Installation, maintenance, and upgrades for electrical systems in commercial buildings.
  • Lighting Design & Installation: Design and installation of energy-efficient lighting solutions to meet your business needs and budget.
  • Data Cabling & Network Infrastructure: Structured cabling systems for voice, data, and video to ensure reliable network connectivity.
  • Electrical Code Compliance Inspections: Regular inspections to identify and rectify any electrical code violations, ensuring safety and code adherence.
  • 24/7 Emergency Electrical Services: Prompt response to unexpected electrical emergencies to minimize downtime and keep your business running smoothly.
